Welcome to Starlit Cafe!


We are a cozy and pastel-themed gaming cafe where coffee lovers and gamers unite under the stars. Whether you're grinding levels or just grabbing your daily latte, this is your third place — a dreamy space to be yourself.

Inspired by galaxy vibes and powered by espresso (and love ❤), Starlit Cafe is designed for connection, comfort, and caffeine. From Nintendo Switch lounges and PC gaming stations in the back, to social corners and work tables in the front we offer a space for everyone that blends the softness of stardust with the energy of a digital arcade.
